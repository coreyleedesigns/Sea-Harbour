Vancouver has gained a reputation for being the Chinese food capital of North America. Traditional Cantonese cuisine using exquisite food preparation techniques is undoubtedly a perfect match with fresh West Coast seafood such as geoduck clams and Alaskan king crabs. Prepared with Sea Harbour's modern culinary concepts using less oil, sugar and salt, these Chinese cuisine delicacies are ready to offer a perfect dining experience for all.
